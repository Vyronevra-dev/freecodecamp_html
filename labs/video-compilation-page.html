Opium Members

Opium is an American record label and collective founded by Playboi Carti in 2019, known for its dark, experimental rage rap sound and aesthetic. The key artists signed to the Atlanta-based label include Ken Carson, Destroy Lonely, and the rap duo Homixide Gang (Beno! and Meechie)

Playboi Carti

Jordan Terrell Carter (born September 13, 1995/1996), known as Playboi Carti, is an influential American rapper and singer from Atlanta, recognized for his avant-garde style, "baby voice" technique, and pioneering role in the rage rap subgenre. He is known for high-fashion aesthetics and founded the Opium label.

Ken Carson

Ken Carson (Kenyatta Lee Bettis Frazier Jr.) is an American rapper and record producer from Atlanta, Georgia, signed to Playboi Carti’s Opium label. Known for his work in the "rage" subgenre, he gained prominence through SoundCloud hits, his debut album Project X, and the critically acclaimed A Great Chaos.

Destroy Lonely

Destroy Lonely (Bobby Wardell Sandimanie III) is an American rapper and singer signed to Playboi Carti's Opium label, known for his melodic trap style and atmospheric fashion. Rising from the Atlanta underground, he gained popularity with projects like No Stylist and the hit single "If Looks Could Kill".
